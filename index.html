About Our Studio

Where Beauty Meets Excellence

Located in the heart of Accra Kwabenya, BeautyPlus Studio is a sanctuary of beauty and relaxation. Our mission is to provide exceptional beauty services in a luxurious environment.

Founded by Christopher Oppong, our studio brings together skilled professionals dedicated to enhancing your natural beauty with the latest techniques and premium products.

We believe that beauty is an art form, and every client deserves a personalized experience that leaves them feeling confident and rejuvenated.

Nail Artistry

Lash Extensions
